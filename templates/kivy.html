Kivy

Kivy is a GUI framework for developing Android and iOS apps in Python. In my opinion, Kivy is a little lacking in appearance and funcionality in comparison to Java, Objective C or Swift apps, but it is a great platform for prototyping apps without having to switch languages or dig too deep into either platform.

From time to time I use Kivy to toy around with an Arduino or interact with APIs, and it has a use as a simple platform for in house apps and prototypes when you don't want to contract a mobile developer until you lock in your functionality.

Kivy apps also run fine in a desktop environment, so they can make for a great portable GUI solution when form isn't the number one concern and you may need desktop and mobile functionality. While I am not a Kivy "expert", I can build some functional apps, when need be.
